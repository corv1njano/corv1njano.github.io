How to buy a modded account?

Visit the main page and choose on of the available shop offers. Then click on the button below your chosen offer to get redirect to the checkout form. After your payment has been received, a member of our team will take care of your account and send you the details.

What payment methods are there?

Our service only accepts gift voucher cards from various online services. The voucher cards must of course correspond to the value of the product. Our service only accepts German (regional) voucher cards. We accept the following:
- Amazon Gift Card
- PaySafe Card
- PlayStation/PSN Card
- Xbox Gift Card
